Mission Overview

Purpose-built for reliable autonomous flight

The KL UAS program packages long-range performance, resilient communications, and a flexible ground control station into a single cohesive ecosystem, enabling pilots to focus on high-value mission outcomes.

10" Carbon Cinelifter

🎯

Operational focus

Built around INAV and ArduPilot flight stacks, the system emphasizes dependable controls, redundant power distribution, and robust telemetry. Paired with a Linux-based ground station, it streamlines mission planning from takeoff through data delivery.

Capabilities

Integrated flight hardware and software

From stabilized cinematography to inspection routes, the airframe and electronics work together to maintain smooth, responsive control and capture the data your mission demands.

⚙️

Custom propulsion

High-thrust 10-inch propulsion with tunable PID profiles ensures precise maneuvering even in challenging wind conditions.

🛰️

Sensor fusion

LiDAR, optical flow, and HD FPV video merge to provide situational awareness and accurate position holding.

Technology Stack

Hardware and software ecosystems

A curated blend of open-source projects and custom electronics provides the flexibility to experiment while maintaining mission readiness.

Flight systems

  • INAV & ArduPilot firmware with mission scripting
  • Redundant IMU and barometric sensing
  • GPS/GLONASS positioning with RTK readiness

Electronics

  • High-efficiency ESC powertrain
  • Custom PCB power distribution and filtering

Ground station

  • Raspberry Pi mission computer with QGroundControl

  • Web-based dashboards for fleet monitoring

Software

  • Python automation scripts and diagnostics

Mission Insights

Design decisions that keep pilots confident

Each mission generates new learnings. These highlights summarize the engineering choices that give KL UAS its edge.

Thermal management

Layered carbon chassis routes heat away from the ESC bank while preserving rigidity for cinematic payloads.

Signal integrity

Shielded harnessing and isolated power rails keep sensor noise low, even when capturing high-bandwidth HD video.

Mission resilience

Dual-battery support and automated return-to-home sequences guard against unexpected power or link losses.

Field serviceability

Modular arms, quick-release payload mounts, and labeled wiring simplify repairs between sorties.
